Korean Beef

World's Best Korean Beef

As seen on therecipecritic.com

Description

The best Korean Beef recipie I've found online.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons seasame oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 cups Jasmine rice
  • 2 cups water
  • Frozen pack of Asian Vegitable Medley

Steps

  1. Rinse off rice and combine rinsed rice and water to instant pot. Set instant pot to highest setting for 3 minutes, allowing it to rest for 10 minutes, pressurized, after the 3 minutes are complete.
  2. Cook Asian Vegitable Medley according to package instuction to complete at the same time as the rice finishes.
  3. While the rice is cooking, in a large skillet/wok, cook ground beef and garlic over medium heat until cooked through. Discard any extra fat.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, red pepper flakes, and pepper. Pout over ground beef and let simmer for another 2 minutes.
  5. Serve over rice and top with Asian Vegitable Medley
