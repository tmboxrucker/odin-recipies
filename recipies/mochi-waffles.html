Mochie Waffles

World's Best Mochie Waffles

As seen on joshuaweissman.com

Description

The best Mochie Waffle recipie I've found online.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1.5 cup mochiko flour (my preferred brand: here
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1.5 teaspoon baking powder
  • .5 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 2 tbs gently melted unsalted butter

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk in 1 egg. While whisking, stream in whole milk and gently melted butter. Use a stand mixer for an easier time.
  2. Once combined, whisk in fine seas salt, baking powder, and sugar.
  3. Add in mochiko flour until paste-like batter forms.
  4. Pour mixture into a piping bag.
  5. Preheat waffle iron, spray with cooking spray, and pipe, in a spiral pattern from the center, enough batter to fill ~3/4 of the waffle iron.

This recipie is best topped with bananas foster, syrup, and/or mixed berries.
